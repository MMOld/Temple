Interfacepublic interface IBehavior extends IDestructableEventDispatcher , IEventDispatcher , IDestructable
Implementors AbstractBehavior

A behavior adds functionality to an object, the object becomes the behaviors target. It does not change it target. The implementation of the behavior is derived from the decorator pattern. A behavior is always targeted on one (and only one) object.
